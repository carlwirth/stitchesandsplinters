Meet Kate (and Tinkle)

Kate has been doing all kinds of crafts and seamstress work for more years than anyone is willing to ask her about and still call themselves a friend. She also creates incredible jewelry! Kate and her wonderful husband work together on the amazing wood crafts found in our photo gallery.

Kate is also very active in her local Project-Linus chapter. She will, once again, be participating in their annual Make a Blanket Day. This is a very important organization. Please click our links to visit their webpage, learn more below, and support them.

Meet Project Linus

Near and dear to Kate's heart is Project Linus: A nationwide non-profit which organizes local groups to donate their time building blankets to provide to children for comfort at a critical time of need.

Project Linus is a long standing, non-profit organization providing over 7,595,141 (and counting...) blankets delivered since 1995. There are many ways to give.... You can become a 'Blanketeer' helping cut and sew, donate time at local events or distributing to the community, become a chapter coordinator, or provide always welcome financial support. It has been incredibily rewarding to see the joy and comfort on children's faces when they receive a blanket! To Learn more, visit www.https://www.projectlinus.org
