We are a proud supporter of Project Linus.


"Helping children in need."


Sawdust and Dreams is very active in the local Project-Linus chapter.


Project Linus is a non-profit organization. They provide homemade blankets to children in need. All blankets are lovingly made by adults and children from all walks of life and many different sources.


With chapters in all 50 states, Project Linus continues to grow. Blankets are collected locally and distributed to children in hospitals, shelters, social service agencies, or anywhere that a child might be in need of a big hug.
